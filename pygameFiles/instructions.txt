- use the arrow keys to move the character left and right
- use the space button to make the character jump
- the coins serve as the score. the more coins you get,the higher your score will be.

Level 1: 
avoid the spikes
get the coins
get to the treasure chest
you have 30 seconds to reach the treasure chest
if 30 seconds, the game will end and you will return to the menu

Level 2: 
avoid the spikes
get the coins
get to the treasure chest
you have 20 seconds to reach the treasure chest
if 20 seconds, the game will end and you will return to the menu
